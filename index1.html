STRESS MANAGEMENT


It is not possible for life to go in the direction you want it to go. When the things aren't going your way,it brings pressure to your mind.
Stress can cause a great mental damage to our minds. So how can we reduce , or even prevent our mind from coming under a pressure? there are many ways,such as:
  1. Keep a positive attitude.
  2. Accept that there are events that you cannot control.
  3. Don't rely on alcohol, drugs, or compulsive behaviors to reduce stress.
  4. Seek out social support. Spend enough time with those you enjoy.
